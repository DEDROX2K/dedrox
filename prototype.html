A summary about me

I’m a Creative Technologist and Interaction Designer who thrives on bridging the gap between R&D, design, and engineering. After completing my MSc in UX Design, I moved to the UK specifically to seek out complex, high-impact technical challenges.
At Aircards, I've had the opportunity to tackle exactly that. Working directly alongside our Head of Product, Phil Scales, I operate as a core driver for both product development and client relations. I manage end-to-end project lifecycles, which has involved traveling across London, Scotland, and Ireland to lead on-site research and technical discovery for global brands like Lenovo Business, Red Bull, Guinness, and Newcastle United FC.

Because my work sits right at the intersection of technical R&D and interaction design, my responsibilities naturally extend into technical sales and management. I regularly pitch solutions to stakeholders, gather complex requirements in the field, and translate those raw client needs into tangible, production-ready engineering deliverables.

I would love to connect and discuss how my unique blend of client-facing R&D, technical sales, and interaction design can bring immediate value to your upcoming projects.
