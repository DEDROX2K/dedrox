About

I build bridges between complex technical systems and intuitive human experiences.

My background isn't a straight line—and that is my greatest asset. By blending an MS in User Experience with deep roots in 3D game design and spatial computing, I approach problems from multiple angles. Whether I am architecting a gamified mobile app, prototyping AR/VR environments, or streamlining dense data platforms, my goal is always the same: remove the noise and translate complexity into clear, actionable design.

"Good design doesn't happen in a vacuum. It lives in the exact space between a user's pain points and an engineer's technical constraints. My job is to navigate that space and make the intricate feel effortless."
