Features

Canvas View

Instead of endlessly scrolling through a traditional, linear page full of links, you can freely pan and zoom around an open canvas. This spatial approach allows you to organize your thoughts, projects, and resources visually. It gives you a bird's-eye view of your work, letting you map out information exactly how your brain processes it.

Planning Widgets

Inspired by tools like Figma, we are introducing built-in widgets designed to make your planning process faster and more intuitive. By integrating useful tools directly into your workspace—like quick task lists, trackers, or calendars—you can streamline your workflow. This means less time spent managing the app and more time focused on actually completing your tasks.

Interactive Tiles

Soon, we will be rolling out Interactive Tiles. These versatile building blocks can act as anything you need them to be: a quick text note, a web link with a rich preview, a folder to organize other items, or a "rack" to group related content together. This modular system means you can build out your workspace piece by piece, customizing it to perfectly fit your unique workflow.

UX-Driven Application

Because this application is being built by a UX designer, user-friendliness and accessibility are at the core of everything we do. We aren't just making it look nice; we are actively applying established UX laws and design principles. This ensures the interface is highly intuitive, reduces cognitive load, and provides a seamless, frustration-free experience for everyone who uses it.
