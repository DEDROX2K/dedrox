We made a decision

We have stripped away the 'features' that modern tech uses to colonize your mind—the streaks, the badges, and the social validation. What remains is a pure environment where you can exist without being tracked, analyzed, or nudged. We are not a platform; we are a vault, a workshop, and a sanctuary for the digital self.

Our minds have been taken captive –
by social approval systems, by newsfeeds and timelines, by advertisements and corporate agendas.
