Your bookmarks.
Your drive.

A local-first desktop bookmark and note board built with Electron and React. Open any folder, let AirPaste create a data.json, and keep every card on your own machine.

Download Beta (v0.1.0)
Feature 01

Single canvas per folder

Organize your thoughts and links spatially. Every folder is its own workspace.

Feature 02

Global paste

Instantly capture URLs and plain text snippets. AirPaste handles the integration from your clipboard.

Feature 03

Open Graph previews

Rich visual links with graceful fallback cards for a beautiful, readable board.

Feature 04

Local-first JSON

No database. No cloud sync. Your data is stored in a clean data.json file inside your folder.

Feature 05

Minimal sidebar

Folder controls and local card count indicators. Stay focused on your content.

Feature 06

Desktop-First

Built with Electron for performance and full filesystem access that browsers can't reach.

Collaborative Moodboard

A place to play. Drag and drop stickers to organize your thoughts.
