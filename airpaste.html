Our Manifesto

In a time when every company has a bid on our data, when we exchange our privacy for information, when every move we make is influenced by how others will perceive it, we have little space to call our own.

Our minds have been taken captive –
by social approval systems, by newsfeeds and timelines, by advertisements and corporate agendas.

Our information is scattered across platforms, bound by terms and conditions. What should have never been lost is now yours again. We promise:

  • no social features
  • no vanity metrics
  • no invasive tracking
  • no social pressure
  • no collaboration
  • no ads
