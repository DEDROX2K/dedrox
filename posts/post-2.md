# From Idea to Prototype in One Weekend

I use a fast loop: **problem framing -> rough flow -> clickable prototype -> quick validation**.

## Weekend cadence

- Friday: write assumptions and outcomes.
- Saturday: create low-fidelity flows.
- Sunday: polish, test, and note next decisions.

> Obsidian helps because notes, outlines, and drafts stay in one place.
